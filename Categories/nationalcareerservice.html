National Career Service (NCS)

Categories women Empowerment National Career Service (NCS)

"Empowering youth through employment opportunities."

The National Career Service (NCS) is an initiative by the Government of India to provide a wide array of employment and career-related services. It aims to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers by offering various facilities, including job matching, career counseling, vocational guidance, and more. NCS is a step towards creating a unified platform that brings together employers, job seekers, training providers, and other stakeholders.

Launched by the Ministry of Labour & Employment, NCS offers a transparent and user-friendly online system that facilitates registration and job search across India. It also provides resources like career guidance, skill development courses, and internships, making it a comprehensive tool for job seekers and students alike.

Necessary Document to apply for NCS

To avail of the services provided by NCS, individuals need to register on the portal. The basic documents required for registration include:

  • Aadhaar Card or any other ID proof
  • Educational Certificates
  • Resume
  • Passport-sized photograph

The official website for the National Career Service provides all the necessary tools and information for job seekers and employers alike.
