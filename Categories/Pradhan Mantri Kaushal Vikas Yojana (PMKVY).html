Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Categories Employment Schemes Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is the flagship scheme of the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE).

The objective of this skill certification and reward scheme is to enable a large number of Indian youth to take up industry-relevant skill training that will help them in securing a better livelihood.

Key Features of PMKVY:
o Short-term training provided to unemployed youth.
o Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) to certify individuals with prior experience.
o Special focus on Kaushal and Rozgar Mela, which mobilize the community.
o Placement assistance provided to candidates upon successful completion of training.
o Training aligned to National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF).
o The government pays training fees directly to training providers.

Necessary Document for Task

Eligibility:
1. Indian nationals who are unemployed or school/college dropouts.
2. Candidates above the age of 18.
3. Special provisions for women, SC/ST, and differently-abled individuals.
4. Individuals who have previously undergone skill training can also apply under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL).

Benefits:
1. Monetary rewards on successful completion of skill training.
2. Job opportunities through placement assistance.
3. Skill certificates recognized by the industry.
4. Opportunity to learn new skills or upgrade existing ones.

About PMKVY

PMKVY was launched by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship in 2015, with the aim of providing quality training to Indian youth to meet the global demands of skilled labor. The scheme is implemented through various training partners and centers across India.
