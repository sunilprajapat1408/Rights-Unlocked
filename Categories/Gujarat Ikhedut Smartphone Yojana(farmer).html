Gujarat Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana

Gujarat Government is Committed to provide Benefits to the Farmers of the State through various Government Schemes of the State. Today we are Going to Discuss the Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana 2024, under which Smartphones will be given to the Farmers of the State. Read this Article for Ikhedut Portal for Smartphone Scheme, Ikhedut Portal 2024 Yojana List and Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana 2024.

Objectives Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana 2024:
• To Provide Financial Assistance on purchase of Smartphones.
• To Empower Farmers of the State.
• To Empower Digital Literacy in the State.
• To Provide Financial Assistance of Rupees 6,000 or 40% of Purchase of Smartphone, whichever is less.

Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana 2024 Eligibility Criteria:
• Applicant Farmer must be Permanent Resident of Gujarat State.
• Application can be made on First come, first Served Basis.
• Smartphones Will be Provided Once in Lifetime.
• Beneficiaries cannot Reclaim it.
• Only Farmers of the State are Eligible to Apply for the Scheme.

Documents Required:
• Farmer’s Registration
• Aadhar Card
• Mobile Number
• Bank Account
• Domicile Certificate
• Passport Size Photo
• Land Records Documents

Gujarat Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana

Gujarat Ikhedut Smartphone Yojana provides smartphones to farmers, enabling them to access agricultural information, services, and market prices. This scheme aims to empower farmers with digital tools for better productivity and informed decision-making.
