Travel and Health Insurance

Securing travel and health insurance is essential for a safe and worry-free study abroad experience.

Purchase comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage. Health insurance is also mandatory for international students in many countries. It covers medical expenses, including hospital stays, doctor visits, and prescriptions.

Ensure your insurance policies are valid for the entire duration of your stay abroad. Keep copies of your insurance documents and emergency contact numbers with you at all times.

Necessary Document for Task

  • Proof of Identity (Passport)
  • Proof of Admission (Offer letter from the university)
  • Travel insurance policy documents
  • Health insurance policy documents
  • Emergency contact information
