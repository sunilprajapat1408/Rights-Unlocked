Non-Recourse Loans

[ Non-recourse loans limit the lender's ability to recover dues to only the collateral provided. ]

Non-recourse loans are secured loans where the lender's recovery is limited to the collateral used for the loan. The borrowerâ€™s other assets are protected from being seized in case of default. These loans are common in real estate and project financing.

The primary advantage for borrowers is the reduced personal liability. However, these loans often come with stricter eligibility criteria and higher interest rates to compensate for the increased risk to the lender.

Necessary Documents for Task

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar card, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility bills, Rent agreement)
  • Proof of Income (Salary slips, Income tax returns)
  • Detailed project report (for project financing)
  • Property or asset documents
  • Bank statements
  • Photographs of the applicant
