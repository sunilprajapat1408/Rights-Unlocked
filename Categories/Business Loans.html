Business Loans

Business loans provide financial assistance to entrepreneurs and businesses without requiring collateral.

Business loans are unsecured loans aimed at supporting the financial needs of small and medium enterprises (SMEs). These loans can be used for business expansion, purchasing equipment, inventory management, or working capital needs. Approval is based on the business's financial health and creditworthiness.

Interest rates on business loans are higher than secured loans due to the lack of collateral. The loan tenure ranges from 1 to 5 years, with flexible repayment options. Quick processing and disbursal make business loans a viable option for immediate funding requirements.

Necessary Document for Task

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar card, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility bills, Rent agreement)
  • Business registration certificate
  • Financial statements (Balance sheet, P&L statement)
  • Bank statements
  • Income tax returns
  • Photographs of the applicant
