Aapki Beti Hamari Beti

Aapki Beti Hamari Beti is a Haryana State Government Scheme in which a sum of Rs 21000 is invested with Life Insurance Corporation LIC in the name of 1st Girl child of SC/BPL families and 2nd child of family belonging to any caste. On attaining 18 years of age, the girl child will be paid a tentative With effect from 24.08.2015 third girl child born in families belonging to any caste were also covered.

Objective :
The objective of this scheme is to improve child sex ratio and to ensure survival, proper health and promote education of girls in the state. Every girl child has a right to be born and have access to education in order to develop her potential.

Benefit Provided :
Rs. 21000/-(One time is invested in LIC of India). Till now 460347 beneficiaries have been covered. During the current financial year 2023-24, 35932 beneficiaries have been covered upto the Month of October, 2023. A sum of Rs. 26798.50 lakh is provided in the budget for the year 2023-24, out of which a sum of Rs. 8805.56 lakh has been spent upto the month of November, 2023.

Documents Required :
1. Parivar Pehchan Patra Number
2. Age Proof: Birth Certificate 3. Resident Proof/Address Proof/Ownership Proof (Anyone of the following):
● Ration Card
● Voter ID
● Electricity Bill
● Telephone Bill
4. Caste Certificate (Only in case of SC)
5. BPL Certificate (Only BPL Families)
6. Tikka Report/Immunization Card for verification of timely immunization

Aapki Beti Hamari Beti

This service is to apply for Aapki Beti Hamari Beti from Women and Child Development Department. This is a G2C service. Applicants need to register to apply for this service. Individuals can apply for this service.
