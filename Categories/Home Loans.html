Home Loans

[ Home loans are financial assistance provided by banks or financial institutions to individuals for purchasing or renovating a home ]

Home loans are secured loans where the property purchased serves as collateral. These loans offer competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options. Home loans are usually long-term, ranging from 5 to 30 years, allowing borrowers to repay in manageable monthly installments.

The loan amount depends on the property's value and the borrower's repayment capacity. Banks may finance up to 80-90% of the property's market value. Home loans are available for different purposes, including buying a new home, constructing a house, or renovating an existing property.

Necessary Document for Task

• Proof of Identity (Aadhar card, Passport, Voter ID)
• Proof of Address (Utility bills, Rent agreement)
• Proof of Income (Salary slips, Income tax returns)
• Property documents (Title deed, Sale agreement)
• Bank statements
• Photographs of the applicant
