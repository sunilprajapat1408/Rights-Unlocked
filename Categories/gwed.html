Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation (GWEDC)

Categories Scholarship Empowerment Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation (GWEDC)

"Empowering Women for Economic Independence"

The Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation (GWEDC) is an initiative by the Gujarat government aimed at promoting the economic empowerment of women. Established with the vision to create a robust support system for women entrepreneurs, GWEDC focuses on providing financial assistance, skill development, and marketing support to women in various sectors. This organization plays a crucial role in uplifting the socio-economic status of women across Gujarat, enabling them to become self-reliant and contributing members of society.

Through various schemes and programs, GWEDC offers grants, loans, and training opportunities tailored to meet the needs of women from different backgrounds, including rural and urban areas. The corporation is committed to fostering an environment where women can thrive economically, ensuring gender equality and inclusive growth in the region.

Necessary Documents for Availing GWEDC Benefits

To apply for the schemes offered by GWEDC, the following documents are typically required:

  • Identity Proof (Aadhar Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Address Proof (Ration Card, Utility Bills, etc.)
  • Income Certificate
  • Business Plan/Proposal
  • Bank Account Details
