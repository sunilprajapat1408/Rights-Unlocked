CBSE Udaan Scholarship Program

Introduction
o CBSE UDAAN Scheme is the major welfare scheme of CBSE for Girls Students.
o It was launched in the year 2014.
o Central Board of Secondary Education is the nodal agency of this UDAAN Scheme.
o Ministry of Education is the Nodal Ministry of this scheme.
o The main objective behind launching this scheme is to increase the enrolment of girls students in technical educational institutes.
o CBSE UDAAN Scheme gives an opportunity to girls students to pursue their career in engineering courses.
o Under CBSE UDAAN Scheme, CBSE will guide and help the enrolled Girls Students to prepare for the Engineering Entrance Examination.

Documents required:
• The below mentioned documents are needed at the time of applying for CBSE Udaan Scheme :-
o Passport Size Photo.
o Aadhar Card.
o Residence Proof.
o Birth Certificate.
o Caste Certificate.
o 10th Marksheet.
o 10th Certificate.

Benefits of the Scheme:
o Free Tablets with Pre Loaded Content for Study.
o Free Online or Offline Classes.
o Study Material for Preparation.
o Tutorials and Lecture Videos.
o Virtual Contact Classes at City Centers.
o Motivation Sessions.
o Student Helpline Services.
o No Admission Fees and Tuition Fees if secured admission in IIT, NIT or Prestigious Engineering College

CBSE Udaan Scholarship Program

The CBSE Udaan Scholarship Program is one of the best government schemes for girls administered by the Ministry of Education. This is a scholarship program launched by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) with the aim to help meritorious female students of economically vulnerable families in India to obtain their higher education from prestigious engineering institutions.

    Frequently Asked Questions...?

    The program is open to girl students studying in Class 11 in CBSE-affiliated schools. Students must be enrolled in the Science stream with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) as core subjects and must have scored a minimum of 70% in Class 10 with at least 80% in Mathematics and Science.

    The program provides free access to study materials, tutorials, and virtual mentoring sessions. Participants receive support through assessments, practice tests, study groups, and doubt-clearing sessions. Additional financial support is offered to top-performing students for higher education.

    Students can apply for the Udaan Program online through the CBSE Udaan portal. The application process requires the submission of personal details, academic records, and a recommendation from the school principal. The selection is based on academic merit and socio-economic background.

    No, the Udaan Program is open to girl students from all over India, provided they meet the eligibility criteria and are enrolled in a CBSE-affiliated school. The program aims to reach girls from various regions, including underprivileged and rural areas.

    The program offers a comprehensive learning package, including online tutorials, weekly assessments, and study modules. Mentorship is provided by experts in science and engineering fields, along with personalized guidance to help students strengthen their concepts in PCM subjects.

    Yes, top-performing students in the Udaan Program are eligible for additional financial scholarships that support their higher education in engineering. The financial aid is intended to cover tuition fees, books, and other educational expenses in their college years.
