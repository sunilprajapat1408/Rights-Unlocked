Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana (MMUY)

Categories Women Empowerment Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana (MMUY)

"Empowering Women, Uplifting Families"

The Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana (MMUY) is an initiative aimed at providing financial support to women in Gujarat. Launched by the state government, this scheme seeks to empower women by providing them interest-free loans for various self-employment activities.

Under this scheme, women can form joint liability earning and saving groups (JLESGs) to avail loans up to Rs. 1 lakh without any interest. This initiative not only aims to support women's economic activities but also to encourage entrepreneurship among women, fostering a spirit of self-reliance and economic independence.

Necessary Document for MMUY

To apply for the Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana, the following documents are required:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Residence Proof
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • Photographs of Group Members
  • Details of the Business Plan

Gujarat Government

The official website of the Gujarat Government provides detailed information about the Mukhyamantri Mahila Utkarsh Yojana (MMUY), including eligibility criteria, application process, and benefits.
