Education Loans

Education loans provide financial assistance to students for pursuing higher education in India or abroad.

Education loans are unsecured loans that cover tuition fees, accommodation, books, and other educational expenses. These loans are provided based on the studentâ€™s academic performance and the earning potential of the course they are enrolling in.

Repayment typically begins after the completion of the course, with a moratorium period for finding employment. Interest rates are competitive, and the loan tenure can extend up to 15 years. Education loans offer tax benefits under Section 80E of the Income Tax Act.

Necessary Document for Task

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar card, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility bills, Rent agreement)
  • Admission letter from the educational institution
  • Fee structure of the course
  • Academic records
  • Bank statements
  • Photographs of the applicant
