National Beekeeping & Honey Mission

The National Beekeeping & Honey Mission aims to promote holistic growth of the beekeeping industry for increased income and employment. It focuses on enhancing honey production, providing infrastructural support, and empowering beekeepers through advanced technologies and skill development.

National Beekeeping & Honey Mission Objectives:
 1.Holistic Growth:
 o Promote beekeeping for income and employment generation.
o Provide livelihood support to farm and non-farm households.
o Enhance agricultural and horticultural production.
2.Infrastructure Development:
o Develop quality honeybee nucleus stock and bee breeders.
o Establish disease diagnostic labs, Integrated Beekeeping Development Centres (IBDCs), Centres of Excellence (CoEs), and equipment manufacturing units.
o Build postharvest and marketing infrastructures (e.g., honey processing plants, storages, branding, and marketing centers).
3.Quality Control:
o Set up state-of-the-art quality control labs at regional and district levels for honey and beehive products testing.
4.Technological Advancements:
o Implement blockchain/traceability systems for honey and beehive products.
o Use IT tools in beekeeping, including online registration.
5.Honey Corridors:
o Develop and facilitate honey corridors in potential areas.

Documents Required
1. Aadhaar card
2. Land documents
3. Bank details
4. DPR
