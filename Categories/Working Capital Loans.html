Working Capital Loans

Working capital loans provide businesses with the necessary funds to manage day-to-day operational expenses.

These loans are designed to finance a company's everyday operations, such as paying salaries, purchasing inventory, or managing cash flow. They are short-term loans that help businesses maintain smooth operations without facing liquidity issues.

Working capital loans can be secured or unsecured, depending on the borrower's creditworthiness and business performance. Interest rates and repayment terms vary based on the lender’s policies and the business’s financial health.

Necessary Document for Task

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar card, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility bills, Rent agreement)
  • Business registration certificate
  • Financial statements (Balance sheet, P&L statement)
  • Bank statements
  • Income tax returns
  • Photographs of the applicant
