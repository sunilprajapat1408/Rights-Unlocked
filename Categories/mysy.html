MYSY Scholarship

Categories Scholarship Schemes MYSY Scholarship

"Empowering youth through the Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana"

The Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana (MYSY) is a scholarship scheme introduced by the Government of Gujarat to support students in their higher education pursuits. The scheme provides financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections to help them pursue undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses.

The MYSY scholarship covers various streams including engineering, medical, pharmacy, architecture, and other professional courses. The aim is to ensure that financial constraints do not hinder deserving students from achieving their educational goals.

Necessary Documents for Application

The following documents are typically required for applying to the MYSY scholarship:

  • Completed application form
  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, passport, etc.)
  • Proof of residence
  • Mark sheets and certificates of previous academic qualifications
  • Income certificate
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank account details
  • Recent passport-sized photographs

MYSY Scholarship Portal

For detailed information and to apply online for the MYSY scholarship, visit the official MYSY Scholarship Portal.
